Patient-Logger

Collaborated with multiple team members to design and develop software for clinical sensor to log data for patients.

Purpose of software product:

The purpose of this software product is to help doctors and medical assistants with logging and understanding how their patients’ symptoms change with environmental conditions and factors.

Tools Used

  • Kivy
  • Pyrebase/Firebase

Features of this software product:

  • Starting Screen
  • Log In
  • Patient List
  • Patient Page
  • New Patient Sign Up
  • Data Page

Features I'm responsible for:

  • Patient List
  • Patient Page

I’ve committed two major patches within GitHub where I setup the foundation of two features, the patient list and patient page. Within the patient list, I generated and displayed an amount of buttons according to the amount of patients. Within the patient page, I implemented a method to read and display patient information.
